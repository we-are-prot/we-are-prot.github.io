Our process

Generally we focus on three early stages of start-ups. A Proof of concept to get your core idea into code and see if the basic premise is feasible. Not all project need this but this step highly reccomended. Next, a Prototype will let you check you assuption of your target market before investing a substantial amount of resources in the final product. It's also a great way to demontrate your product to future investors. Then there is the Minimal viable product, which will contain the simpelest feature set possible that is still useful enough to charge your customers.
